Mission Brief

Why Sentinel Watch exists

Most defence-forces content online is either recruitment marketing or dry PDF notes. Sentinel Watch sits in between: readable dispatches on service life, current affairs, and exam strategy — written by an aspirant, for aspirants preparing for CDS and OTA.

Who it's for

  • CDS & OTA aspirants who need GK and current-affairs material that's actually organised by topic.
  • NDA hopefuls curious about day-to-day life before they commit to the path.
  • Anyone interested in the Indian Armed Forces beyond headline news.

What you'll find here

  • Exam Prep — pattern breakdowns, strategy, and study plans.
  • Army / Navy / Air Force — service-specific explainers and life-at-the-academy pieces.
  • Current Affairs — the defence news that tends to show up in GK papers.
